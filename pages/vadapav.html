Vadapav

🛒 Ingredients:

  • ✔4 boiled potatoes
  • ✔1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • ✔2 green chilies, ginger, curry leaves
  • ✔Salt, turmeric, and oil for frying
  • ✔6 pav rolls
  • ✔Water as needed

👩‍🍳 Recipe:

  1. Make potato mixture with spices and form balls.
  2. Prepare a besan batter
  3. Dip them in besan batter and deep fry till golden.
  4. Cut pav, spread chutneys.Place vada inside.
  5. Place vada inside.
