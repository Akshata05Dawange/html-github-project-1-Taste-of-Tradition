1. Dal Baati Churma

Dal Bati Churma is a traditional Rajasthani meal consisting of three main components: a savory lentil stew (Dal), hard baked wheat bread balls (Bati), and a sweet, powdered wheat-based delicacy (Churma). It is a popular dish for special occasions and is known for its combination of rich, savory, and sweet flavors, and its hearty, rustic nature.
