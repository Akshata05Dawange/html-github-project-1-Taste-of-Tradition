🍛 Punjabi Chole & Bhature Recipe 🍽

🛒 Ingredients for Chole:

  • ✨ 1 cup kabuli chana (chickpeas)/
  • 🧅 1 onion, 2 tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 🧄 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 🌿 1 tsp cumin seeds (jeera), 1 bay leaf (tamallpatra)
  • 🌶 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 🥄 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 🔥 1 tsp garam masala
  • 🧂 Salt to taste
  • >🛢 1 tbsp oil

🍞 Ingredients for Bhature:

  • 🌾 1½ cup maida
  • 🥛 2 tbsp curd (dahi)
  • 🧂 Pinch of salt
  • 🧈 1 tbsp oil
  • 🧁 ½ tsp
  • 💨 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 💧 Water as needed

👩‍🍳 Recipe:

  1. → Soak chana overnight and boil it for 4–5 whistles.
  2. → Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, bay leaf, onion, and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. → Add tomatoes and all dry spices. Cook till soft and aromatic.
  4. → Add boiled chana and little water. Cook for 10–12 minutes.
  5. → For Bhature – Mix all ingredients, knead soft dough, rest for 2 hours.
  6. → Roll into medium-size puris and deep fry till golden and fluffy.
  7. → Serve hot with spicy chole, onion rings, lemon wedges & pickle 😋
