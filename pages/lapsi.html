⭐Lapsi⭐

🌟Ingredients for Lapsi

  • Broken wheat (daliya/fada) – 1 cup
  • Ghee (clarified butter) – 3 to 4 tablespoons
  • Jaggery (gur) – ¾ cup (or sugar as per taste)
  • Water – 2½ cups
  • Cardamom powder (elaichi) – ½ teaspoon
  • Chopped nuts – almonds, cashews, or raisins (optional)

👩‍🍳Lapsi Recipe

  1. Roast the wheat – Heat ghee in a pan, add the broken wheat, and roast on low flame till golden brown and aromatic.
  2. Boil water – In another pan, boil 2½ cups of water.
  3. Cook the wheat – Add the roasted wheat to the boiling water. Cover and cook on low flame until soft and water is absorbed.
  4. Add sweetness – Mix in grated jaggery (or sugar) and stir until it melts completely
  5. Flavor and garnish – Add cardamom powder and mix well. Garnish with chopped nuts.
  6. Serve hot or warm – Lapsi tastes delicious when served warm with a spoon of ghee on top!
