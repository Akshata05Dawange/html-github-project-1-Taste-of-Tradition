Welcome to Maharashtra Recipes

Here are some of the most famous dishes of Maharashtra:

1.Puranpoli

Puranpoli is a traditional Maharashtrian sweet flatbread filled with jaggery and chana dal mixture.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chana dal
  • 1 cup grated jaggery
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Water as needed

Method:

  1. Boil and mash chana dal.
  2. Cook it with jaggery until thick. Add cardamom and cool.
  3. Knead dough from flour, ghee, and water.
  4. Stuff dough with filling, roll gently, and cook both sides with ghee.

2.Vada Pav

Vada Pav is Mumbai’s most popular street food — a spicy potato fritter inside a pav (bread roll).

Ingredients:

  • 4 boiled potatoes
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 2 green chilies, ginger, curry leaves
  • Salt, turmeric, and oil for frying
  • 6 pav rolls

Method:

  1. Make potato mixture with spices and form balls.
  2. Dip them in besan batter and deep fry till golden.
  3. Cut pav, spread chutneys, and place vada inside.

3.Modak

Modak is a sweet dumpling loved by Lord Ganesha, made from rice flour and stuffed with coconut and jaggery.

Ingredients for Filling:

  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 3/4 cup jaggery
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder

Ingredients for Dough:

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp ghee
  • Pinch of salt

Method:

  1. Cook coconut and jaggery until thick.
  2. Boil water with ghee and salt, add rice flour, and mix well.
  3. Knead dough, make small cups, fill with mixture, and shape modaks.
  4. Steam for 10–12 minutes.
