Modak

🛒 Ingredients:

  • ✔ 1 cup grated coconut
  • ✔3/4 cup jaggery
  • ✔1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • ✔ 2 cups wheat flour
  • ✔1 cup water
  • ✔1 tsp ghee
  • ✔Pinch of salt

👩‍🍳 Recipe:

  1. Cook coconut and jaggery until thick.
  2. Boil water with ghee and salt.
  3. add rice flour, and mix well.
  4. Knead dough, make small cups, fill with mixture, and shape modaks.
  5. Steam for 10–12 minutes.
