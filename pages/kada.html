🍯 Kada Prasad Recipe

🛒 Ingredients:

  • 🥣 1 cup wheat flour (gehun ka atta)
  • 🧈 1 cup ghee (pure desi ghee)
  • 🍬 1 cup sugar
  • 💧 3 cups water

👩‍🍳Recipe:

  1. → In a heavy pan, heat ghee on medium flame.
  2. → Add wheat flour and roast continuously till it turns golden brown and gives a nice aroma.
  3. → In another pan, boil water and add sugar. Stir till sugar dissolves completely.
  4. → Slowly pour the hot sugar syrup into the roasted flour mixture (carefully — it may splutter).
  5. → Stir continuously till the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  6. → Once it becomes smooth and shiny, turn off the gas.
✨ Serve hot as Kada Prasad — with devotion and love. 🙏
