🌿 Sarson da Saag and Makki ki Roti 🌽

🛒 Ingredients:

  • ✔ 500g Sarson (Mustard leaves)
  • ✔ 250g Spinach (Palak)
  • ✔ 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  • ✔ 2 Green chilies
  • ✔ 1 inch Ginger
  • ✔ 4 tbsp Maize flour (Makki ka atta)
  • ✔ Salt to taste
  • ✔ 2 tbsp Ghee or Butter

👩‍🍳 Recipe:

  1. → Wash and chop the mustard leaves and spinach.
  2. → Boil them in water with turmeric until soft.
  3. → Grind green chilies and ginger into a paste and add to the boiled leaves.
  4. → Simmer for 10-15 minutes and mash well.
  5. → Mix in maize flour to thicken the saag.
  6. → Cook for another 5 minutes, add salt, and top with ghee or butter.
  7. → Serve hot with Makki ki Roti.
