West Bengal – The Sweet Capital of India

West Bengal, especially Kolkata, is famous for its traditional sweets and street foods that reflect the rich culture and heritage of Bengal.

Famous Dishes of Kolkata

  • Rasgulla : Soft, spongy balls made from chhena and soaked in sugar syrup.
  • Mishti Doi : Traditional sweet curd prepared with caramelized sugar or jaggery.
  • Kathi Roll : A popular Kolkata street food made with paratha and spiced fillings.

Rasgulla

Delicious Rasgulla from Kolkata!

Rasgulla is a beloved Indian sweet with a rich history, cultural significance, and delicious taste, originating from the eastern part of South Asia.


Misthi-doi

Sweet and creamy Mishti Doi!

It is not just a dessert but a feeling that is in every Bengali household and festival. The smooth texture, rich sweetness, and subtle tanginess render it an evergreen dessert.


Kathi-roll

Kolkata’s iconic street wrap!

The word “Kathi” means stick/skewer in Bengali, referring to the original kebab sticks used in making the rolls.
